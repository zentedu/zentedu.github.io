Game Development History

  • 1998-2002

    Idea Development

    The developer of the game, Riot Games, was co-founded by Brandon "Ryze" Beck and Marc "Tryndamere" Merrill, who were roommates when they attended the University of Southern California. They teamed up with Steve "Guinsoo" Feak, former designer of Warcraft III: Frozen Throne Defense of the Ancient custom map, and Steve "Pendragon" Mescon, the administrator of the former official support facility for map, to develop League of Legends!

  • October 7 2008

    Published Release

    League of Legends is born", when some DotA community members believe the game is so exciting and creative that it represents the emergence of a new genre and deserves to be the game. Its own professionalism with significantly enhanced features and services around the game

  • October 27 2009

    Officially Released

    In League of Legends, each player is called a "summoner", controlling a character, called a "champion" (Champion - differentiated from Hero in Dota 2 and God in SMITE), to Face off against a team of other players or computer opponents

    Top Game
